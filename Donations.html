    So as to remain a true and proper fan-based convention, True Self con will be using only money gathered by its runners and via donations in order to operate. This naturally means that donations, no matter how small, are of vital importance to the convention. Those donations will go almost exclusively to venue space for the first several years, although if enough is gained beyond needed space, the convention will then start polling to see what extras the attendees might like to see; Things like adding additional rooms, looking into perhaps getting guest cosplayers, catering, etc.

    For the first year the convention is mostly focused on getting together enough funding to hold a 2 day, 1 room convention with various vendors/artists and some fun activities. Our goal for year one as far as donations go is $1500.

    While this seems like a great deal of money, with everyone’s help it won’t be much at all. As an easy break-down, the first-level tier donation (netting you a special “2026 Donator” badge is $20 - to reach $1500, we would only need 75 total donations. Yet if everyone decided to go to the highest tier ($100 and multiple extras), it would only take 15. Mix and match it all and I’d say we can reach our goal easily enough!

    Total donations will be reported once a month beginning at the end of February, so please look around and consider if you would like to donate or not! Donations from each individual will be kept track of, meaning if you'd like to hit the highest tier, you don't have to do it all at once!
