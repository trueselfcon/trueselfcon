    Escape Room FAQs

    Why is this an RSVP event and not open to all?

    As this event requires a certain amount of set up, knowing the numbers coming will allow us to make sure everyone has room to move around as they try to uncover the way out.

    Why does it cost $50?

    That $50 goes towards venue costs, any food or water served on site and to assist in getting items for the event itself.

    If the cost is for all that how will you be raising money for the convention?

    Simple. We will be holding a raffle after the escape room and also have a handful of items for sell. All RSVP tickets come with 3 free Raffle Tickets, so even if you don't want to spend more you have a chance to win! And yes, additional tickets will be sold at the event.

    Will we really be locked in?

    Not at all. Participants will be able to come and go at any time - they're just asked to believe that they are in a locked room while trying to solve the puzzles.

    Will there be food and drink?

    We will provide small snacks to munch on (cookies, candy, crackers, etc) and will have bottled water available. There is also a McDonald's in the same parking lot as the venue, so if everyone decides we can call ahead and order from them and have lunch after the escape part is done. NO ALCOHOL PLEASE!

    Do I have to be in costume? Does it have to be Persona 4?

    No and No. Although costumes are encouraged, what game of the various series spawned from SMT (including Persona) you choose to wear a costume from is entirely up to you! Or just come as yourself and enjoy!

    What items will be for sale/raffle?

    Various! If you keep an eye on this page, I will be posting them as we get them in and ready. If we have enough people buy raffle tickets on site (or through this page) we may even add more.
